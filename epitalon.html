What people mean by Epitalon

Epitalon is often discussed in longevity, cellular-aging, telomere, pineal, sleep, and circadian-rhythm conversations. Public interest is high, but claim discipline is critical.

Mechanism conversation

Common discussion points include telomerase/telomere theory, circadian signaling, pineal peptide research, oxidative stress, and cellular regulation.

Evidence caution

Aging-related claims are easy to overstate. Avoid promises about lifespan, reversal of aging, disease prevention, sleep correction, or immune enhancement.

Best education angle

Frame Epitalon as a longevity-research conversation and clearly separate theory, preclinical discussion, and human clinical evidence.
