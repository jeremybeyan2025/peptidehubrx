Tirzepatide

Mounjaro® / Zepbound®

Tirzepatide is marketed as Mounjaro® and Zepbound®. Education should discuss the dual incretin conversation, screening, nutrition support, and medical supervision.

Liraglutide

Victoza® / Saxenda®

Liraglutide is marketed as Victoza® and Saxenda®. The education angle is expectations, tolerability, daily administration context, and provider-guided selection.

Dulaglutide

Trulicity®

Dulaglutide is marketed as Trulicity®. It belongs in education around glycemic health, medication history, metabolic-care settings, and clinician oversight.

The Metabolic Support Stack

The safest public-facing “stack” discussion is not adding more compounds. It is protein adequacy, resistance training, hydration, sleep, micronutrient awareness, side-effect education, and provider monitoring.

Safety topics patients should understand

Common education topics include GI side effects, hydration, constipation, nausea, lean-mass preservation, gallbladder/pancreas warnings, medication interactions, pregnancy considerations, and medical-history screening.
