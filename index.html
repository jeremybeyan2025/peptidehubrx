Do you prescribe peptides directly?

This website is structured as an intake and education funnel. Prescribing decisions must be made by a licensed provider after appropriate review.

Are peptides right for everyone?

No. Eligibility depends on health history, medications, goals, labs when appropriate, and provider judgment.

Can you guarantee results?

No. Outcomes vary and no wellness or medical program should guarantee results.

Is this medical advice?

No. The content is educational and does not replace medical advice from a licensed clinician.
